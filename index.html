Traditional Baechu Kimchi

Recipe

  1. Salt napa cabbage and let it soften.
  2. Rinse and drain well.
  3. Prepare chili seasoning.
  4. Add radish and green onions.
  5. Coat cabbage evenly.
  6. Pack into a clean container.
  7. Ferment at room temperature.
  8. Refrigerate and enjoy.

About Kimchi

History

Kimchi has been a cornerstone of Korean cuisine for centuries, traditionally made through fermentation.

Main Ingredients

Napa cabbage, gochugaru, garlic, ginger, and fermented seasoning.
